Working efficiency and popularity

DOWEll'S Electro Were has many achievements to its credit. It manufactured plant type contact switches for the first time in India in 1961. It later diversified Into electrical connection and development 5,000 types of terminals and joint accessories. It also Exported cable terminals, connectors for copper and aluminium conductors and hand operated hydraulic crimping tools, as well as non - ferrous metal semis like copper and aluminium flats, strips, rods, tubes Etc.

Dowell's Expertise



Termination plays a vital role in power generation, distribution & transmission. Failures are witnessed in end joint due to improper fitment. poor workmanship, lack of knowledge, non-availability of suitable Instruments for cable end termination.

Dowell's with Its accumulated experience and constant innovation in terminal technology and their quality standard products have provided wise solution to their end customers.


Dowell's History

Dowell's History The Company was first established in sltabuldi, Nagpur in 1950 by Mr. Patel and started producing Cycle Rickshaws. Mr. Patel with his vast engineering background, subsequently diversified the company into manufacturing: Ster-delta Starters In 1954.

Switch gears (introduced for the first time In India by us) in 1958. Time switches, Centrifugal Switches for Motors, Components of KWH Meters and Push-Button Switches for fans in 1961.
