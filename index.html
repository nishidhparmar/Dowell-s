DOWEll'S Electro Were has many achievements to its credit. It manufactured plant type contact switches for the first time in India in 1961. It later diversified Into electrical connection and development 5,000 types of terminals and joint accessories. Dowell's Expertise

Switch gears (introduced for the first time In India by us) in 1958. Time switches, Centrifugal Switches for Motors, Components of KWH Meters and Push-Button Switches for fans in 1961.
